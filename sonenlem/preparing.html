Preparing for the Trip

Skiing to the North Pole is a physical challenge. In preparation for the trip, the expedition leader set up a training program that consisted of walking on uneven terrain with various obstacles. One was climbing a 580 meter hill with weights in a back pack. Another challenge was to tow a tire on its side with a harness over nine kilometers several times a week. The team has walked an accumulated a distance of over 250 kilometers in preparation for the trip over the last ten weeks.
