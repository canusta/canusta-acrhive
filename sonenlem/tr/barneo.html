Barneo

Barneo her sene Nisan başında 89. Enlemde kurulan ve buz akıntısı ile sürüklenen bir buz istasyonu. Yaklaşık üç hafta boyunca açık kalan bu istasyon Nisanın son haftası kapanıyor. Barneoda Antonov 72/74 uçaklarının inebileceği 1200 metrelik bir pist bulunuyor.
Barneo özel bir kuruluş tarafından işletilip Rus Coğrafya Derneği tarafından destekleniyor.

İMAJLAR İÇİN AŞAGI İNİN
TERIBEAR is not just a cute red teddy. It’s a superhero teddy bear designed to help abandoned children longing for a family. TERIBEAR is a fundraising project of the Tereza Maxová Foundation in the Czech Republic. Its central aim is to raise awareness and address the serious issue of institutional care for children. Although the situation is slowly improving, the Czech Republic still has the highest number of disadvantaged children growing up in orphanages in the European Union. One of the long-term goals of the Tereza Maxová Foundation is to allow as many children as possible to grow up in a family environment instead of institutions. By purchasing TERIBEAR products, you support the Tereza Maxová Foundation activities aiming to help and promote foster family care in the Czech Republic.
